Stewardship for London homes that aren't for the open market.

NourNest Private Residences looks after homes that are not listed publicly. Director-led, NDA-standard, retainer-based.

Private Residences is the opposite of monetisation. We hold the keys, run the schedule, and keep the home ready — quietly, consistently, and without surprises.

One director-led relationship. No call centres. No franchise model. No platform between you and the person responsible for your home.

Who we look after

The owner who lives there. Your principal London residence, kept running while you travel, host, or simply prefer to delegate the operating work.

The owner who visits. A pied-à-terre held in your name or your family office's name. Ready on a day's notice. Cared for between visits as though you were arriving tomorrow.

The developer with new prime stock. Handover-to-resale or handover-to-let stewardship. Snagging completion, compliance, presentation, and viewings handled by one accountable manager — not a rota of contractors.

What stewardship looks like

Day-to-day

  • Compliance calendar — gas, electrical, fire, water, lift, insurance
  • Maintenance scheduling and vendor oversight
  • Housekeeping rotation tailored to occupancy pattern
  • Seasonal FF&E switching, wardrobe rotation, soft-furnishing care
  • Discreet move-in, move-out, and between-visit reset

Concierge, on request

  • Airport meet and greet, private transfer coordination
  • Household staffing coordination — housekeepers, drivers, nannies, tutors
  • Pre-arrival grocery and in-residence preparation
  • Restaurant reservations, ticketing, member-club liaison
  • Art handling and storage liaison
  • Vehicle storage and movement
  • Wine cellar management
  • Private chef and event coordination

Bespoke works

  • Project-managed refurbishments, large or small
  • Single-vendor accountability on multi-trade jobs
  • Quiet, scheduled-around-you delivery

How we work

A single director-level relationship. Gerda Micke, Director, is your point of contact and the person accountable for every decision and every vendor on your home.

NDA-standard discretion. Every team member, contractor, and vendor signs to the same confidentiality standard. We do not publish client names, addresses, or photography. We do not feature your home in marketing.

Quarterly stewardship reporting. A written report on what was done, what is upcoming, what was spent, and what is recommended. Optional integration with your family office's reporting standard.

24/7 on-call line for urgent matters. Answered by someone empowered to act, not a routed message.

Transparent, retainer-based engagement. No commission on works, no kickbacks from vendors, no hidden margins. You see what we spend.

Our standard of care

Seven years operating prime central London residential property, director-led from day one.

A vetted vendor network built case by case, not bought in. Trades who have worked with us repeatedly, hold our discretion standard, and turn up when they say they will.

Audit-ready operations. Every payment, every compliance certificate, every contractor visit is logged and retrievable.

Quiet, consistent, accountable. The stewardship equivalent of a senior estate manager — without the in-house headcount.

Engagement

  1. Initial conversation. Thirty minutes. No obligation. We discuss the property, the brief, and whether we are the right fit.
  2. Property walk-through. A site visit and stewardship plan tailored to the residence, your occupancy pattern, and your priorities.
  3. Onboarding and contract. Clear scope. Clear retainer. Signed NDAs. Vendor introductions where helpful.
  4. Live stewardship. Day-to-day operations begin. Monthly check-in by phone or in person, however you prefer.
  5. Quarterly review. Written report, recommendations for the quarter ahead, and a recalibration of scope if anything has changed.
