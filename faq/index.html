FAQ

The most common guest questions, answered.

Twelve questions we get most often. If your answer isn't here, message us — hello@nournestapartments.com or WhatsApp +44 7802 666 672.

What time can I check in?

Standard check-in is from 3pm. Earlier check-in is available on request when the apartment isn't occupied the night before — just ask via WhatsApp or at booking. We use smart locks or lockboxes for self-check-in, so late arrivals (after 10pm) are no problem.

What time is check-out?

Standard check-out is 11am. Free late check-out until 2pm is available subject to availability — just message us the day before. Direct bookings get priority on late check-out requests.

Do you allow pets?

Selected apartments welcome dogs. A small number also accommodate cats. There's a £25 pet fee per stay covering extra cleaning. Filter the listings page or message us before booking — we'll point you to the apartments that say yes.

Is there a discount for long stays?

Yes. Stays of 7+ nights get a weekly rate (roughly 10-15% lower than the nightly equivalent). Stays of 28+ nights get a monthly rate (roughly 25-35% lower). Council tax is included on stays over 90 days. We quote on enquiry — message us with your dates.

What's included in the apartment?

Every NourNest apartment includes: fully equipped kitchen (cookware, dishwasher, kettle, coffee), fast Wi-Fi (200+ Mbps), washing machine, dryer (most apartments), hotel-grade towels and bed linen, smart TV with Netflix, workstation-ready desk and chair.

Direct bookings include a welcome hamper with local treats.

How do I pay and what's the cancellation policy?

Card payment at booking via our secure system. For stays under 14 nights: full refund if cancelled 7+ days before check-in. For stays of 14-30 nights: 14 days' notice required. For stays of 30+ nights: 30 days' notice. The exact terms for your booking are confirmed at reservation.

Is parking included?

Depends on the apartment. A handful have allocated parking; most central London flats have street parking with daily resident permits available (typically £5-15/day). We list parking options on each property page and can advise before booking.

Can I smoke in the apartment?

No smoking inside any NourNest apartment. Smoking outdoors is fine where there's a balcony, garden, or terrace. A cleaning recovery fee applies if indoor smoking is detected.

Are your apartments licensed for short-let?

Yes — every NourNest apartment complies with London's 90-night short-let rule and the relevant council licensing where required. We handle the compliance side; you don't need to worry about it as a guest.

What if something goes wrong during my stay?

WhatsApp us on +44 7802 666 672, day or night. Most issues are sorted within the hour during the day and overnight if it's urgent. For genuine emergencies (water leak, no heating, lockout) we have on-call cover.

Can I bring kids? Do you have cots and high chairs?

Yes — most NourNest apartments are family-friendly. Cots, high chairs, baby monitors, and stair gates are available on request at no extra charge. Just ask when booking or message us at least 48 hours before arrival.

Why book direct with NourNest instead of Airbnb or Booking.com?

Three reasons: (1) no platform service fees, so the same apartment costs less on our site. (2) Direct bookings include the welcome hamper and priority on late check-out. (3) You're talking to us directly via WhatsApp instead of through a platform. Same apartment, better experience, lower total cost.
