Privacy Policy

NourNest Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller for personal data processed via nournestapartments.com. Registered in England & Wales, company number 16629708.

What we collect

  • Contact form submissions: name, email, phone (optional), message content.
  • Booking enquiries: guest details required to confirm reservations (handled in part by BOOM, our property management system).
  • Property management enquiries: details you choose to share about your apartment or property.
  • Website analytics: minimal, anonymised page-view data (no personally identifiable tracking).

Why we collect it

  • To respond to your enquiry (contractual / legitimate interest basis).
  • To deliver booking and stay services (contractual basis).
  • To meet legal and tax obligations (legal basis).

Who we share data with

We share personal data only with processors necessary to deliver our services:

  • BOOM (BoomNow Inc.) — property management system, holds booking and guest data.
  • Email and communication providers (Google Workspace) used for hello@nournestapartments.com.
  • Hosting and analytics providers (Cloudflare) — minimal, anonymised traffic data.
  • Accountants and legal advisors, only where required.

We do not sell personal data. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

How long we keep it

  • Contact enquiries: 24 months after the last contact.
  • Booking records: 7 years for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Property management agreements: term of agreement plus 7 years.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these, contact us at hello@nournestapartments.com and we'll respond within one month.

Cookies

This site uses minimal essential cookies only. We do not run third-party advertising or behavioural tracking cookies. Where booking is handled by the BOOM platform, their own cookie policy applies.

Complaints

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your data, you may complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.
