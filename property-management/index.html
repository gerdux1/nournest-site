We work with a small number of London landlords to manage their apartments end-to-end. Director-led. Transparent fees. No franchise model.

What we manage

One person accountable. The full stack handled.

We take a London apartment, photograph it properly, list it on direct channels and the major travel platforms, set the pricing, look after every guest who stays, manage the cleaners and trades, keep the compliance up to date, and report to you quarterly on what's happened.

You see what we spend. You sign one agreement. You speak to one person.

Our terms, plainly

  • Management fee: 17-20% of gross rental income, agreed up front based on apartment, location, and scope.
  • No commission on works. If your boiler needs replacing, you pay the trade directly. We don't add a margin.
  • Term: 12-month rolling agreement, 60 days' notice either side.
  • Reporting: monthly statement, quarterly written review.
  • Onboarding: typically 7-14 days from agreement signed to first guest booking.

What we look for in an apartment

  • London location, ideally well-connected to public transport.
  • Short-let licensing in place (or willingness to arrange).
  • Furnishing standard suitable for short-let (we can advise or upgrade where needed).
  • Owner who's looking for a partnership, not just the cheapest fee.

How to start

  1. Drop us a message via the contact form, email, or WhatsApp.
  2. We arrange a thirty-minute call to understand your apartment and what you want from management.
  3. Property walk-through (or virtual if you're not in London).
  4. Written proposal — fee, scope, projected yield, onboarding plan.
  5. Agreement signed, onboarding begins.
